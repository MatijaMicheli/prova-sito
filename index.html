F.A.Q.

What is special about Indoor Drone Tours services?

We are a premium content service with a team of experts to execute your video or photography projects. We have created thousands of drone tours nationwide and work with top brands like CBRE, Lowes, the Chicago Bulls, and more.

How do your drones navigate indoors?

Our drones are flown by experienced and licensed first-person-view (FPV) drone pilots. Our inside drones are flown using virtual reality goggles that can see what the front of the drone sees allowing them to navigate room to room.

How do you create an indoor drone video?

Use a professional drone videography company like Indoor Drone Tours to create your indoor drone video. Be sure to hire a reputable drone videography company that has a proven portfolio of drone tours and had licensed and trained drone tour pilots.

How do you use a drone tour?

You can distribute your drone tour across all of your marketing efforts. Use your drone tour in email marketing, on social media like LinkedIn and Instagram and through direct communications like text messages.

How do you deliver my drone tour?

I produce high quality drone tours and deliver you the final video files. We can also host your drone tour using our free property website pages. You will be able to upload the video file of your drone tour to any social media platform.
